Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

BigTextDisplay ("we") runs bigtextdisplay.com. We collect minimal data to operate the service and send optional emails you request.

What we collect

  • Email & first name — when you ask us to email a display link or opt into reminders.
  • Display URLs — links you save via email capture (stored to power "My displays").
  • PayPal — if you subscribe to Pro, PayPal processes payment; we receive subscription status and email via webhooks.
  • Local storage — your display settings stay in your browser unless you share a URL.

Email

We use Resend to send transactional emails (your links, invites, optional weekly reminders). You can unsubscribe from reminders by replying to any reminder email.
